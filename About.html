About Me

I'm a driven and enthusiastic software developer with a strong foundation in programming and a passion for crafting innovative solutions. As I complete my final semester of a Associate's degree in Information Technology, I'm eager to apply my skills and knowledge to real-world projects.

With several years of programming experience under my belt, I've developed a solid understanding of various languages and frameworks. I'm constantly seeking new opportunities to learn and grow, staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies.

Beyond my technical expertise, I'm a dedicated individual with a strong work ethic and a collaborative spirit. I enjoy working in teams and believe that diverse perspectives lead to better outcomes.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the world of coffee, savoring new brews and appreciating the artistry behind each cup. I'm also a lifelong fan of orcas, fascinated by their intelligence and beauty. This passion for nature and creativity inspires me in my work as a developer.

I'm an avid music lover, enjoying a wide range of genres and artists. I also find joy in raising chickens, appreciating their unique personalities and contributions to our little farm. And when I need a break from the digital world, I turn to the classic tabletop game Battletech, immersing myself in strategic battles and epic campaigns.

I'm excited to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to projects that make a positive impact. Let's build something amazing together!
